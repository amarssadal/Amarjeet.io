By using Dynamic Race Geometry on the Veltrix Frameset, Cinelli has ensured that all riders are placed in the optimum position for both power and aerodynamics, regardless of frame size, by maintaining the ratio of top tube to seat tube. The Columbus carbon monocoque design balances all-day comfort with power transfer to produce a frame that can happily come through on the chaingang one day and then take part in a hundred-mile sportive the next.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION
  • Frame - Columbus carbon monocoque
  • Fork - Columbus Disc 1 1/8" - 1 1/2" monocoque
  • Bottom bracket - BSA 68mm
  • Calliper brake compatible
  • Internal cable routing
  • Seatpost - 31.6mm
  • Frame weight - 1,200 grams
  • Fork weight 460 grams
