A high-performance saddle for the high-mileage rider, this padding-free throne uses its innovative, lightweight shape and design to keep you poised and positioned for the podium.

Aimed at the experienced road runner, this stunning Selle Italia seat weighs in at only 63g and features a massive SuperFlow cut-out to eliminate pressure on your sensitive areas. Super-strong thanks to its 100% carbon fibre shell, it gives day-long support to your pelvis and sit bones, with its narrow nose giving plenty of thigh clearance to keep your pedal stroke as smooth as possible.

TECHNOLOGIES

Carbon Wear: Tensile carbon strength fibre and innovative Carbon Wear technology mix the best carbon properties with a special protective film that ensures maximum resistance.

SuperFlow: The SuperFlow technology drastically reduces all physical limitations caused by prolonged pressure in the perineal area, especially for long distances. It allows an optimal and comfortable riding position.
