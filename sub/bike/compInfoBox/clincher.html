The DT Swiss PRC 1400 SPLINE 65mm Clincher Wheelset pairs a deep carbon rim with 240 hubs and premium spokes to make a rapid set of race-ready wheels. The 36-tooth ratchet freehub provides near-instant engagement for attacking the line or powering out of corners whilst the braking performance has been improved by using a new resin polymer for greater power and modulation. The rims have an internal width of 18mm, making them perfect for use with 25mm tyres, and they are tubeless compatible too.

Please note: The manufacturer does not recommend using latex inner tubes with carbon clincher wheels. Using latex tubes may void your warranty.
