Developed to be aero, the new Alanera offers the full cable and wire integration including an easy fixing system for Di2 unit under the stem.

Specific aero spacers with an innovative mounting system complete the design of the handlebar when assembled on the bike, resulting more integrated with the frame. A special aero topcap and the integrated Garmin® bike mount compatible with Garmin® 500 and 800 series complete the accessories line.

The optmized design of stem and handlebar and the RHM shape guarantees a very compact reach and drop for a fast change from the shifters to the drops.

  • Material: Carbon
