Ready for clocking up the miles, the Selle Italia SP-01 Boost Tekno Superflow Saddle gives you fantastic comfort and control on your road bike.

Perfect for racing, this road bike saddle features a strong carbon rail construction, which means that you'll benefit from its exceptional strength to weight ratio. Selle Italia has also equipped this road-ready saddle with a carbon shell, which gives you even greater weight savings without sacrificing strength. Weighing in at only 117g for the small version and 120g for the larger version, this saddle is the ultimate choice of upgrade for riders who want superior stiffness without any unnecessary weight.
