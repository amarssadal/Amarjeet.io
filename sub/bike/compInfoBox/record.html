The Campagnolo Record 11-speed Chain has been designed by Campagnolo's engineers to work perfectly with the 11 speed Super Record and Record groupsets but of course, it can be used with any Campagnolo 11 speed drivetrain.

It features a unique method of connection that requires a specific tool. This precise closing method has been tested by the professional racing teams that Campagnolo supplies.

You must use tool UT-CN300 to ensure total safety and comply with the terms of Campagnolo's warranty.

FEATURES
  • Hollow pins
  • Specific profile side plates
  • Smooth shifting
  • Dedicated joining pin
