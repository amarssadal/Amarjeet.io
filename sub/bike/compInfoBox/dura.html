The Dura-Ace R9170 Di2 Groupset sits at the pinnacle of Shimano performance in every way. Di2 electronic shifting for exceptional, precise shifting efficiency & superior hydraulic disc brake performance to give you the edge on demanding routes. Designed to raise the bar for drivetrain performance, the R9170 groupset is a favourite among World Tour riders thanks to its unmatched shifting performance and advanced function. Both the front and rear derailleur communicate with each other as you shift up and down the gears to ensure that the perfect balance between cassette sprocket and chaining selection is being used; this balance provides the most efficient and performance enhancing power-transfer available.

Superior Hydraulic Disc Brake Performance This groupset is equipped with among the most advanced hydraulic disc brakes in the world of road cycling today. Shimano has been long-known for their superb mountain bike disc brake systems which have now been developed and optimised to provide the highest levels of brake performance available in the world of competitive road cycling.
